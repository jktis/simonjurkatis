Work in Progress

Inferring Trade Directions in Fast Markets. Bank of England Working Paper No. 896

Proposes a new method to classify trades into buyer and seller initiated trades. The new method improves classification accuracy particularly at low timestamp granularity. For example, for data timestamped to the second misclassification rate is reduced by half compared to the popular Lee-Ready algorithm. While the established algorithms rely on an ad hoc assignment of trades to quotes to classify trades, the new algorithm searches for the quote that matches a trade.
